White-label capabilities can be of five different types:

full-stack capability
A white-label capability providing both backend services and front-end components (e.g., micro-frontends). Most white label capabilities in actor domains are full-stack capabilities.
service
A white-label capability providing a backend services via an API.
complex integration
A white-label capability providing a backend services that integrates with a complex external system (e.g., Salesforce or Workday).
development/design
A white-label capability providing foundational components needed to build other capabilities.
other
Other white label capabilities, such as infrastructure.

About the Logo

The logo of this app is derived from the "slicing the elephant" visual frequently used in Brent's presentations on Tech Strategy (e.g., slide 7 here).

Slicing the elephant phrase describes methods to make big challenge (creating a pan-European, multi-brand, multi-actor marketplace platform) into a series of smaller challenges (the White Label Capabilities roadmap).
